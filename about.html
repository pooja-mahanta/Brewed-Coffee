Our Story

A Cup Above the Rest

It all started in 2015 when Arjun and Meera Kapoor, two coffee-obsessed friends from Ludhiana, decided the city deserved better than instant coffee and loud chain cafes. They leased a tiny 400 sq ft space on Coffee Lane, painted the walls in warm mocha tones, and opened Brewed & Beloved with just six tables and a dream.

Today, we've grown to serve over 12,000 customers, won multiple awards, and become the city's favourite third place ‚Äî not home, not work, but somewhere warm in between.

We source our beans ethically from farms in Karnataka, Colombia, and Ethiopia, and every cup is brewed to order by our trained artisan baristas.

Our Journey

How We Grew

2015
The Beginning

Opened our first 400 sq ft cafe on Coffee Lane, Ludhiana with 6 tables and a second-hand espresso machine. Sold out on day one.

2017
First Expansion

Knocked down the wall next door, doubled our seating, and launched our first signature cold brew series.

2019
Award-Winning Roasts

Won "Best Specialty Cafe in Punjab" at the North India Food Awards. Started sourcing directly from Ethiopian farms.

2022
Sustainability Pledge

Eliminated all plastic, switched to fully compostable cups and packaging. Partnered with local farms for our food menu.

2024
Going Digital

Launched online reservations, our loyalty app, and began training 12 new baristas for our second location coming in 2025.
